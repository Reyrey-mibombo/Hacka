Features

Everything you need,
all in one bot

From free shift tracking to enterprise AI forecasting — STRATA grows with your server.

🎟️

Advanced Ticket System

Fully customizable ticket panels with staff claim, feedback forms, DM routing, and transcript logs.

⏱️

Shift Tracking

Staff log shifts with /shift_start and /shift_end. Points awarded per 5 minutes. Streak system with trophies.

🚀

Auto-Promotion Engine

Checks 9 real requirements every 15 minutes. Owner gets a DM with Approve/Interview/Deny buttons.

📊

Real Analytics

Live dashboards with engagement rates, burnout detection, weekly/monthly reports, and growth tracking.

🛡️

Advanced Moderation

Ban, mute (timeout), and audit logs with real Discord API. DM notifications, activity logging, and mod channel posts.

🔮

AI Forecasting

Linear regression on real activity data predicts 30-day growth trends. No fake numbers — only real DB data.

🏅

Elite Badge System

Grant, view, and list elite achievement badges. Saved to DB, DMs the recipient, and showcases in their profile.

💎

Interactive Dashboards

3-tab live dashboards for Overview, Staff, and Moderation — all with real-time MongoDB data and button navigation.

🌡️

Activity Heatmaps

Visual ASCII heatmaps showing real 24-hour and day-of-week activity patterns from your server's data.
